Hi my name is rezika and I have some advice for you!

Keep up and learn something new every day

It is liking learning a new language. If you hop-on hop-off for long periods, you may lose some skills and tricks you had learned some time ago. So, be consistent and build up upon your knowledge. As far as possible but your skills in practice.

Nothing is like seeing your work alive on the web.

There are plenty of sources out there. Skill crush is one of the best but everyone has something new to say or at least a different prespective that may enlighten you

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
