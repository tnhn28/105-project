Hi, my name is Krissy and I have some advice for you!

Practice!

You should treat a coding language like any other foreign language. It does not come instinctively and must be practiced constantly to create fluency.

Patience!

It's hard to learn a new concept as an adult and we often put pressure on ourselves to do it quickly and perfectly. That kind of sucks the fun out of it though. Try to be patient as you notice and appreciate progress, as slowly as it may happen.
