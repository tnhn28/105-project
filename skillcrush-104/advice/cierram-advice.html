Hi my name is Cierra, and I have some coding advice for you!

Confusion is normal.

Look at learning code like you would a new language. There are rules you've never heard of before, culture differences... And as you learn new things in tech (and even when you're well-versed in a tech topic), YOU WILL STILL EXPERIENCE CONFUSION! And it's okay!

Confusionis a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It's a signal that you have engaged in the problem solving pursuit that is math and technology and that it's time for you to dig deeper and keep going because clarity awaits.

Start NOW (and use the One Tab Chrome extension to speed up your computer).

Fear is an ugly thing, but it also can be turned around and made the fuel to your success if you push through.

Everything seemed to have gotten in the way of me learning how to code over a year and a half ago, so I gave in. But now I'm ready and determined to work past any bumps in the road.

It also helps that I found a way to speed up my computer: One Tab Chrome extension! A lifesaver.

Google is your best friend!

It feels weird when you're constantly looking up answers--like you should know more than you do. But it's VERY common to need to research answers. Embrace it!
