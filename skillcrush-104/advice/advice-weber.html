My advice for the newbies on Skillcrush*

  • Go at the pace you need. I was determined to blow through Phase 1 in less than the time noted, but then my hard drive died and life got in the way.
  • At the same time, don't stay away from progressing in the work for too long. If you wait too long, each time you come back you'll need to re-learn some of things you already went over.
  • Admit when you're tired and probably coding poorly. Step away when really frustrated. A break is probably just what your brain needs.
  • Find a quiet place to work. This might be at home, but it might be at a coffee shop, or in the library, or in that weird conference room at your day job where nobody wants to be so you get to have it all to yourself.
  • Note: said quiet place may very well include headphones and your favorite jams.
  • Keep at it. If a whole bunch of other Skillcrushers have crushed it already, we can too.
  • If you've actually read this far, say hey! at jweber617 [@] gmail.com

*This is hilarious to me as I still consider myself a newbie.
