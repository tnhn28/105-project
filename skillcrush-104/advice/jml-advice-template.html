Hi my name is Jeannine and I have some advice for you!

Start with what you know...

The most important lesson for me has been to embrace challenges.

When things don't work it's an opportunity to learn.

At that moment I need to breathe deep, stop the panic inside

and the voices that tell me that I can't do this..

I just start with what I know and do that.

Then I do the next things I know and soon enough...

I have built something!
