Hello, my name is Kelsey & I have some advice for you!

Expect the Unexpected.

So you're like, a really good problem solver and pretty darn technically savvy... Everyone and their mom seems to be coding these days, you should just do it! Basic HTML & CSS are a breeze and you're really enjoying the challenge!

And then you begin working with floats. Suddenly you're doubting every decision you've ever made in your life that lead up to deciding to stupidly teach yourself how to be a web developer.

Coding is hard sometimes; but, then again, so is life. So with coding, just as in life, always be prepared for the unexpected. Sure, you may spend today face-planted on your desk, but hey... You really enjoy the challenge ;)!

Believe in Yourself.

Okay but really, while you inevitably will spend some days facedown and out, this is a completely normal part of the process. The important part is taking the time to process and getting back at it!

We do learn best by failure, after all. So don't be afraid o fail a lot and know that it's part of a deeper, more thorough learning process. Believe in those failures, believe in yourself.
