Hi my name is Leigh and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!
(Especially about the CSS Box Model)

Don't forget about your Developer Tools. Use them to explore what's really going on with those pesky margins and paddings.

Don't rush!

Take your time in each lesson. Don't be in so big of a hurry to finish that you don't take the time to understand every step.

This is especially important when working with Git and GitHub
