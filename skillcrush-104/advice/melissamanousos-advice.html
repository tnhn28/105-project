Hi, my name is Melissa -

I have some advice for you!

Welcome to your journey of constant learning!

It won't be easy at first. As you learn more, things will become second nature. Some concepts will come easily to you while others will leave you stumpped.

Don't be afraid to ask for help. Read a lot. Learn how to google for answers and decypher replies on StackOverflow.

Keep track of your progress.

I highly recommend keeping a journal of some sort for your coding sessions. The format outlined in the 100 Days Of Code challenge is a great template.

Pay special attention to the things that are challenging for you.

Look up additional documentation, practice problems, or explanations of those. Keep working to understand them and asking questions until you do.

If you do track your progress you'll be amazed to look back on previous entries outlining concepts or techniques that baffled you a few short weeks or months before.

Just keep going!

Your life now consists of learning new things all the time. Don't be afraid of it. Everyone was new once.
