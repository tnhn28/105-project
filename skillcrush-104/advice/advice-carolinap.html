Hi my name is Carolina and I have some advice for you!

Believe in yourself!

Yes, you are a newbie, but guess what? We've all been there. Just know that those who persist stop being newbies at some point and become experts eventually.

You can too! But first, you have to get pass the 5 stages of grief.

  1. Denial - I am NOT a newbie. Newbies don't even know the difference between ul and ol and I DO!
  2. Anger - WHYYYYY!!!??? Why am I such a newbie?
  3. Bargaining - Maybe if I make a list of all the things I know, I will prove to everyone I am not a newbie.
  4. Depression - I am the newest of newbies, and I will never not be.
  5. Acceptance - I am a newbie, and that is ok. I will be less of a newbie everyday.

That wasn't so bad, was it?

Now that that part is over, you can focus on the learning, having fun while doing so, and getting a good night sleep because that is when your brain will process the information you learned throughout the day!

Sleep is very important, but most importantly, you have to believe in yourself. Learning a new skill is never easy, if it were, it wouldn't be learning. You have to create those new paths on your brain! So give yourself a break and just keep at it! You'll get there eventually.

DON'T STOP! Believin'!

Trust the process and trust yourself! You can do anything you set your mind to!
