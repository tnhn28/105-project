Hi my name is Carrie Tannahill and I have some advice for you!

Don't get overwhelmed!

When you start programming it is so easy to get overwhelemed with not knowing how to do everything. It is best to always take things one step at a time.

If you are always looking at the big picture, it can be very easy to get overwhelemed. Make sure you just relax and do the next task in front of you.

Don't let perfectionism stop you!

It is easy to let pefectionism get in the way of taking the next step. Letting yourself try new things and fail is a big part of the learning process. Let learning from your mistakes be part of the fun!

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

Trying new things and not being afraid to fail is the only way you can make progress and change the future!
