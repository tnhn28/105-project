Hi, my name is Kiara
and I have some advice for you!

Boldly ask your questions!

Don't shy away from asking a question. I know it sounds cliche, but there really is no such thing as a dumb question when you're learning something new. Plus, it's the only way that you will learn and advance. So be bold and ask away!

In tech, even when you don't have it you still got this!

I remember feeling frustrated and, yes, even a little like an idiot when I struggled on certain lessons, or couldn't figure something out. But I soon learned that such is the nature of the tech field - you try and fail and fail again until you either get it, or make a whole new discovery. So don't get down on yourself. Even at times when you don't feel like you got this, you really do. Keep at it!
