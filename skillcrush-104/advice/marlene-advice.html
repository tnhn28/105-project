Hi my name is Marlene and I have some advice for you!

You can do it!

When you start programming your first web app you are doing something that you have never done before. It's definitely a challenge and sometimes you feel like it is too difficult.

The trick is not to give up. You must keep trying and eventually it will make sense.

Repetition

Doing it over and over is an important part to gaining an understanding.

Practice new and different things until you are comfortable. Push yourself.

Don't give up.
