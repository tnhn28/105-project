Hi my name is Nadine and I have some advice for you!

It's not too late to learn something new.

It would have been easier to stay in healthcare. But five years in, I just knew I was capable of more. With a seven month old sleeping upstairs, I signed up for Skillcrush and never looked back.

I'm proving to myself that I'm capable of so much more than I even realized. I'm shaping my future and the future of my family.

And hey, I can code while breastfeeding!

Having a future job with flexibility is my main priority as we grow our family.

I want to do what I enjoy and have the luxury of seeing my kids grow up, day by day.

So far I'm loving my courses and was shocked to find out that I already knew way more HTML and css than I'd even realized!
