Hi, I'm Jennifer and I have Some Coding Advice for You

A few things I've learned so far...

When you first begin to code, be patient with yourself and take your time.
Learning to code is much easier to absorb when you really take the time to learn it.

Take excellent notes, stay organized, and always make time to PRACTICE.

Try and figure out a coding problem on your own first - this is helpful in learning from your mistakes.
But, don't be afraid to ask for help if you're at your wit's end!

Learn from others and find people in the field that you admire.

Never underestimate your potential and ability to be an amazing coder.

When you begin your professional coding journey, find a mentor, stay present in your coding community, and continue to learn!
