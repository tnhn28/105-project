Hi my name is April, and I have some advice for you!

Practice for fun.

I think it's awesome to do the exercises, but the learning can't stop there. Repetition is what solidifies your memory. So, bored? Code! Dream up a make-believe project. See if you can get it done. You'll surprise yourself with what you know.

You're going to get lost and confused, and that's okay!

Sometimes, things don't go exactly as you think they will. Sometimes, you'll work a tutorial exactly the same way, but you'll get errors or unexpected results.
Awesome!!

This is how you grow! Google the errors. Retrace your steps. ASK for help! Get it figured out. You won't forget the lesson. Promise.
