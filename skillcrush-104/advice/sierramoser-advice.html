Hi my name is Skillcrush and I have some advice for you!

You don't need a 'tech' background to get started!

Don't be scared to try something new! You don't need a background in tech or computers or even design to learn how to code! Take it from me, I have a background in child welfare and office admin for an architecture firm with a Bachelor's of Science in General Studies.

You just need the will to want to do it. I wanted to learn something new and exciting and that is what brought me here to learn how to code.

I'm not saying it isn't scary

I will be the first person to tell you that learning something new is scary, but I like a challenge! Some days I resist a little more to start up a new lesson just because I know it will be harder than the previous one, but I do it!

I push myself to learn difficult things (nothing comes easily to me) and if I can do it then anyone can do it.
