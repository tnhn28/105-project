Maintaining Your Sanity

Perfection is your Frenemy

Stop obsessing over your portfolio design! You are inevitably going to redesign it again when you finish Skillcrush and have improved your skills. Do a "good enough job" and move on!

Try to help someone on Google Groups--even if you feel like an idiot and don't really know what you are talking about. It will encourage other people to participate and more feedback is better than none.

Practice and Forgiveness

Skillcrush is just a starting point. It can only give you a small taste of Photoshop, CSS, Github, etc. Seek out additional resources so you can keep building those skills.

It's easy to get off track--especially if you have attention-sucking children in your house. Forgive yourself when you get off track, but don't derail. Keep going at whatever pace you can manage.
