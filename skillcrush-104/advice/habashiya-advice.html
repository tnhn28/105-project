Hi my name is Hauwa and I have some advice for you!

Youtube and Google are your friends!

When you start programming cross-reference the topics you learn in Skillcrush against what is out there on YouTube and Google.

You'll soon find yourself trying to learn everything. At this point, take a deep breath and only concentrate on what you are currently learning.

Just do it!

Even when it doesn't make sense or doesn't look fancy just do it. You will eventually be able to change things around once you've been through the process a few times. It's better to get started than waiting until everything is perfect.

Walk away when you don't understand anything or find yourself making mistakes. When you come back, you'll be amazed how it all makes sense or how spelling this correctly makes a lot of difference.

Before you know it you'll be a master of your own coding universe.
