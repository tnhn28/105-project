HTML and CSS is your friend!

When you start programming your first web page you are doing something that you have never done before. It's a new challenge, a new opportunity, take advantage of learning this process! You have all the resources that Skillcrush offers at your fingertips.

Confusion may happen but it's a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It's a signal that you have engaged in the problem solving pursuit that is math and technology and that it's time for you to dig deeper and keep going because clarity awaits.

You Can Do This

You have to remember why you started this process, whether its to start a new career or to learn something new and challenge yourself! Remeber why you are doing this!

You may not get everything on the first pass but keep practicing. Your ability to code and your understanding of this new material will only grow! and if your still confused, dont be afraid to ask questions!

The learning process includes learning to train your brain to see and understand something new!
