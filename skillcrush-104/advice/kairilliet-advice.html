Hi my name is Kai Rilliet and I have some advice for you!

Practice as much as you can

When you start out, you are learning a new language. You remember how hard that was in high school. You need to practice as much as you possibly can, without burning yourself out of course.

I thought doing the exercises in Skillcrush was going to be enough, but when it was time to do our first coding project, I was completely stumped.

I wasn't able to do it without using all the resources and guides because I wasn't as familiar with everything as I thought. I needed more practice.

Practice so much that you can do all this with your eyes closed.

Do not get discouraged

It can get overwhelming and feel like you are not making progress, but trust me, you are. You are getting better and better every time you practice.

So don't stop or get discouraged. Do at least one thing everyday to help you with coding and to continue the forward momentum.

And before you know it, you'll be doing it like a pro. And don't forget, you can always use Google.
