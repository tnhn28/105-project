Hi my name is Tyler Kuhn and I have some advice for you!

Never give up!

When you first start your coding journey, you are going to run into alot of problems. You just have to keep trying and checking your code, you'll get it eventually.

You just have to remember you just started, and you dont know very much yet. Soon you will have no problme finding and fixing problems.

Be patient.

I have learned that everything that comes with learning a new skill takes time, you can't rush it or go around it.

Just go at a pace that is comfortable for you, and when you dont understand something spend a little extra time on learning it.
