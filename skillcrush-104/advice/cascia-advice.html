Hi my name is Cascia and I have some advice for you!

Frustration is normal.

If this is brand new to you, don't fret if you find yourself getting frustrated. This is almost like learning a foreign lanuage. With practice it will come.

Sometimes the best thing to do when you're feeling overly frustrated, is to just walk away for a few minutes. Sit down with a clear head and try again. It can really do the trick!

Go Slow.

Rushing leads to mistakes. Mistakes that can be hard to pinpoint if you get too far ahead of yourself! Slow down and write clean code.
