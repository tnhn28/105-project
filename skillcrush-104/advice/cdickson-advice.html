Hi my name is Cassandra and I have some advice for you!

It won't be perfect the first time.

Admit it.. You probably won't actually publish that first website you design. That's ok! The value is in learning the process. Once you have that process down (after a LOT of practice), then you will finally make that beautiful page you've been dreaming of.

Just get started!

Never underestimate the power of small steps taken consistently.
