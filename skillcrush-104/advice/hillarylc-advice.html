Hi my name is Hillary, and I have some advice for you!

Be kind to yourself!

When I started programming I really wanted to know everything, and know it yesterday! I'd get upset if I made a mistake, or forgot how to do something.

I had to constantly remind myself that everything was okay! I had just started learning, and I shouldn't expect to be perfect. I hope you remember that too. Learning a new skill might be difficult and frustrating in the day-to-day, but in 1 month, 6 months or 12 months you will be so much farther than you ever imagined. So stick with it, trust the process, and remember that you can do it!
