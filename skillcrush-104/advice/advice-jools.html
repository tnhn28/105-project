Hi my name is Jools and I have some advice for you!

Have patience with youself!

When you first start to learn programming YOU are going to make mistakes!

You are new to it. It is absolutely normal.You are NOT thick and you are not lacking talent or intelligence. :-)

You learn how to spot your mistakes.

It gets easier with time and practise to spot faulty code and make the right changes.

Sometimes it can hours until find the mistakes.

MAKING MISTAKES AND CORRECTING THEM IS PART OF THE LEARNING PROCESS AND HELPS YOU TO GET BETTER.
